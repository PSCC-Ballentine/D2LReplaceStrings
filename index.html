What are Replace Strings?

Org Unit Strings

  • {OrgUnitId}
  • {OrgUnitName}
  • {OrgUnitCode}
  • {OrgUnitPath}

User Strings

  • {UserId}
  • {UserName}
  • {OrgDefinedId}
  • {FirstName}
  • {LastName}
  • {Email}
  • {ExternalEmail}

Where can you use replace strings?

  • News items
  • Custom widgets
  • Email
  • Intelligent Agents
  • Quizzes
  • Grades comments
  • Quicklinks
  • Dropbox feedback
  • Others?

DO NOT use replace strings in the Content tool!
